Addis Abeba University

Addis abeba University: (Amharic: አዲስ አበባ ዩኒቨርሲቲ) or AAU is an Ethiopian higher institute in Addis Abeba, Ethiopia. The main campus is located in the Arrada Subcity, 6kilo area.

Currently, AAU AAU has enrolled more than 50000 undergraduate (under regular and continuing education proram) and close to 11000 postgraduate students in Applied Science, Social Science, Health Science, and Engineering and other fields. AAU is a University in the making, and much of its short-term plans aim at establishing academic infrastructure and facilities, staff recruitments and man power development so far, we managed to recruit more than 15000 academic staff and adminstrative staff.[1][2]
